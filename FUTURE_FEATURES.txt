# Future Feature Ideas for OpnPage

- Reading Nook: Dedicated space for book lovers to track reading progress, favorite books, reviews, and connect with other readers.
- Gamer Lounge: Gaming-focused dashboard for tracking playtime, achievements, favorite games, and sharing gaming stats.
- Lifting Zone: Fitness area for weightlifters to log workouts, track PRs, routines, and progress photos.
- Runners Hideaway: Running-specific section for tracking miles, routes, races, goals, and connecting with fellow runners.
- More specialized zones for other hobbies and interests as user demand grows.

## Strategy
- Launch core dashboard first to gather feedback.
- Expand with these specialized sections based on user interest and feedback.
- Prioritize features that users request most.
